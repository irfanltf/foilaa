Indonesia’s Food Barn

Ranked as 6th biggest rice producer, 3rd biggest maize producer and 2nd biggest sugar cane producer in Indonesia, Lampung is already a leading regional food production center and will played a significant role in supporting Indonesia’s ambition to became the world’s food barn in 2045

Well Connected Infrastructure

Lampung is the ideal gateaway to two most populous islands and those biggest market in south east asia i.e Java and Sumatera, and its infrastructure is well connected both domestically and internationally through Panjang Port, Bakauheni Port, Radin Inten II international airport and Trans Sumatera Toll Road

Prospective Industrial Estates

Abundance with natural resources and home to 6,1 million working age residents, Lampung offer great opportunity to develop industrial estate.
